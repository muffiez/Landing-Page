Pomeranian Information!

The Pomeranian, as we know it today, descended originally from the Spitz family of dogs in the frozen Arctic region of Iceland.

The original Pomeranians were white, black, brown and parti-colored. The red and orange colors were quite rare at that time.

In 1900 the American Kennel Club recognized the Pomeranian and the American Pomeranian Club (APC) was formed. In 1909 APC was accepted as a Member Club of the AKC and became the designated Parent Club for the breed.

This little compact toy dog does not realize that it is such a small dog. Pomeranians are truly large dogs in a small dog body. They have a vivacious spirit with a foxlike expression. They are playful throughout the majority of their lives, but are also happy to simply hang out on the couch or in your lap.

Pomeranians are like potato chips. You can't have just one.
